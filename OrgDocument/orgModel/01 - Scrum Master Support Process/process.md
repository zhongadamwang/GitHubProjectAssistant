### High-Level Process Steps (Preliminary)
1. **Initialize Support Session**
   - Scrum Master authenticates and accesses assistant
   - System loads team context and current sprint information
   
2. **Identify Support Need**
   - Assistant analyzes current team state and upcoming activities
   - Scrum Master specifies type of support needed
   
3. **Provide Contextual Assistance**
   - Sprint Planning: Generate agenda, facilitate discussions, capture outcomes
   - Daily Standup: Monitor progress, identify blockers, suggest interventions  
   - Retrospectives: Analyze team data, facilitate discussions, track improvements
   - General: Answer questions, provide recommendations, generate reports

4. **Capture and Learn**
   - Record interactions and outcomes
   - Update team analytics and insights
   - Improve future recommendations based on results

## Process Boundaries
**Input**: Scrum Master requests, team data, sprint information
**Output**: Facilitation support, insights, recommendations, reports
**Constraints**: Must align with Scrum framework principles and practices

## Quality Gates
- Support recommendations align with agile principles
- Team privacy and data security maintained
- Actionable insights provided in timely manner
